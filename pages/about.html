ABOUT

"I went to the woods because I wanted to live deliberately. I wanted to live deep and suck out all the marrow of life"

The site serves as the place and method I express thoughts. I experience life by listening, talking and reading -- taking in information at the "front-end". Then I will process the discrete moments at the "back-end" by thinking. Primary output has two forms, blogs written by me and photos taken by another person :)

This is truly quite a personal website, therefore I have little intent to impact other people. But I still want to thank you for your interest no matter what you are.
